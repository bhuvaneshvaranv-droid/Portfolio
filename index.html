About

The analyst behind the reports

I'm an analytical Data Analyst with hands-on experience building interactive Power BI dashboards and KPI reports. My focus is the part that makes a report trustworthy: a clean Star Schema model, reusable DAX measures, and validated data flowing in through Power Query.

With a background in Mathematics and an MBA in HR & Finance, I read business problems and numbers in the same breath. I'm comfortable gathering requirements, shaping the data model, and delivering a dashboard a stakeholder can glance at and know what to do next.

I'm passionate about translating raw data into actionable insights that support real data-driven decisions — and I'm always sharpening my DAX and SQL.
